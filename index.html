About Us

Aplit soft is a full service company.

Our expertise includes software, embedded hardware design, firmware, algorithms and signal processing, prototyping, moving to production, test automation, system security, debug and product testing (QA), web design and more. We help our customers to make appropriate hardware and software choices, reduce product development costs and get faster to the market.

We also offer full turnkey services: You understand the product, we understand the technology. We can take your concept all the way from the idea, through specifications to design and prototypes, till mass production.
